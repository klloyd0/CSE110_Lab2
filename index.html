Agenda

Together we will continue our brainstorming from last meeting on what are the worst locations featured on Kitchen Nightmares. We will be putting these locations on a list that we will then be referencing for our Russian Roulette-inspired game that selects a restaraunt for the player to eat at. Last meeting we chose what kind of restaraunts we want to list, which inevitably led us to the ones featured on the show. We will now be discussing and choosing the worst of the worst we want to include in our game. No Amy's Baking Company - this answer is too easy.

Additional Questions

Why is Amy's Baking Company not allowed?

Everyone knows Amy's Baking Company. It's too easy of a pick.

What counts as 'the worst'?

Normal stuff you'd expect from Kitchen Nightmares - bugs, grime, rats, expired food, horribly crowded menus, horrible staff, you name it!

What if my idea is not good enough?

This is just the brainstorming process! We just want to look for ideas right now. Do not worry and do not overthink.
